Data Table

On this web page you can see a list of people's birthdays that you have saved. At the top of the page is the current full list which includes:

  • a person's ID
  • their name
  • their age
  • their date of birth
  • their preferred cake flavor
  • and whether you have bought their gift yet or not

The age and ID for each person are calculated for you. For each entry you can delete them using the delete button to the right of the entry

Forms

This page also includes 2 forms, a new entry form, and an update entry form. Use the new entry form to add a new birthday you wish to track. To add a new entry just fill out the name, dat of birth in MM/DD/YYYY format, their preferred cake flavor and whether or not you bought them a gift. Finally hit the submit button.

To use the update form, click on the ID of the entry that you wish to update in the table. Their information will automatically fill the update form. You can then change their name, date of birth, preferred cake flavor, and whether or not you boight them a gift. The ID field and Age field are not able to be edited as they are recalculates if necessary. Finally hit the update button to confirm your changes.
